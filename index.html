The art of Software

Developing software is the science of modeling our reality or giving life to our imagination through computer systems using one or more programming languages.

But if we achieve that people recognize the usefulness of our software, enjoy its use, through the plastic, linguistic and / or sound resources used, and additionally we have a structured code that is easy to understand and maintain, beyond a scientific product we have achieved a work of art.

--Guillermo Vitola
